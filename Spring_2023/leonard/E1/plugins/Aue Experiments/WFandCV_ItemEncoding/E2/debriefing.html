The memory test you just took is called recognition; recognition is when you have to decide whether an item or pair of items was experienced previously. It is a task that we use every day. For example, when you answer a multiple choice question on an exam you are performing recognition.

The purpose of this research project is to understand how the characteristics of words influence recognition. We are trying to understand if performance differs for:

Common versus uncommon words
Words experienced in a few sitations versus words experienced in a vairety of situations
Whether the above properties includen memory for individual itesm versus memory for whether or not items appeared together, also know as associative memory.
